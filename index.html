Red roses often symbolize love and passion.Roses come in a variety of colours, including red, yellow, pink, white, and orange.

Bleeding hearts (Lamprocapnos spectabilis) are perennial plants known for their unique, heart-shaped flowers that hang from arching stems. Blooming in spring, they typically feature pink or white flowers and thrive in shaded areas with well-drained soil, making them popular in gardens. Their lush foliage adds texture even after the blooms fade.

Cherry blossoms, or sakura, are delicate pink or white flowers that bloom on cherry trees in spring. Celebrated for their fleeting beauty, they symbolize renewal in Japanese culture and attract visitors during spring festivals. Cherry trees thrive in temperate climates and are popular in parks and gardens.

Dahlias are vibrant perennial plants with large, colorful blooms that flower from summer to fall. They thrive in full sun and well-drained soil, making them popular in gardens and floral arrangements.

Gazania is a hardy perennial with bright, daisy-like flowers in various colors. Thriving in sunny, drought-prone areas, they are low-maintenance and attract pollinators, making them popular for gardens and containers.

Purple orchids are elegant flowers known for their striking purple blooms. Symbolizing beauty and strength, they thrive in bright, indirect light and require careful watering, making them popular as houseplants.
